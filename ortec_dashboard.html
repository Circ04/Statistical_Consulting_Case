Where the elasticity number comes from

After conditioning on bookings-on-books to close the demand→price backdoor, the global elasticity is firmly negative. |β| < 1 means demand is inelastic — revenue grows when prices rise, until capacity binds.

Uplift by market group

Where the projected revenue gains concentrate. NL and DE drive the bulk — both because they have the highest baseline volume and because their historical pricing has more headroom.

Where does the uplift come from?

Market × accommodation type uplift heatmap

Premium accommodations in NL and DE are the single largest revenue lever. Budget tier shows surprisingly little uplift — likely because guests there are price-sensitive (effective β closer to −1) and historical prices were already near optimal.
